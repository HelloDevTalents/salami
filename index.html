The DEVTALENTS team introduces: Salami, a Flutter project bootstrapper. It’s an open-source tool built with mobile app developers in mind. It’ll help us easily begin a new project and will speed up work thanks to process automation. Salami offers a heap of good practices, wrapped in experience, ready to be used.

BENEFITS

Why use the Salami in the project?

Generating mason bricks

We’ve prepared useful mason bricks to skip writing code boilerplate altogether. By using salami_cli, you can quickly generate useful page and widget files. You can also create your own bricks, adjusting your project to your needs and preferences.

Ready-made tools

Salami includes a box of helpful tools, the kinds that a developer will most likely need in every project, such as a first launch callback.

Dividing projects into packages

Salami uses and recommends creating packages with feature domains. This way, your app will be more scalable and well organized.

Salami CLI

The CLI tool we’ve prepared has one mission: to make it super easy for you to manage your flutter project. By generating the app’s code, you reduce the time needed to create and configure it.

Flavor schema

Pre-made flavor schema gets the app ready to take off running in various environments: staging, development and production.

Salami was created based on our own team’s needs. It helps developers speed up repetitive tasks and keeps projects scalable. It ensures that app architecture will be readable and clear, allowing new team members to easily parse your project’s source code. Instead of wasting time on configuring invisible developer options every time, you’ll be able to focus on building real elements of your app. Cohesive and reliable practices are the key to success for any team.

Salami is still growing! If you'd like to join us and help us in improving our tool, you’re welcome to become a project contributor. Take a look at our code of conduct and tips for contributors.

Salami is available for free under a MIT license. Any member of the Flutter community, from anywhere in the world, is invited to use it!
